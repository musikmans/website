{ About Me }

Graduated from the "Cégep de Jonquière" in 2006, I moved to Vancouver soon after to work in the growing IT sector and avoid the harsh East Coast weather. I worked for a few startups doing mostly PHP and ActionScript (Flash) programming until I founded my own company in 2009.

In 2010, I went back to work and was hired by Yellow Pages. In eight years, I occupied several position including Web and Print Designer and Domain Name Specialist (technical support).

In 2018, I decided that technical support wasn't my cup of tea and I left the company. I was looking for a new challenge and I wanted to go back to programming. I decided I would be even more valuable if I upgraded my skills using the latest technologies. So I went back to school and am now even more competent using the latest frameworks and languages.

Now, I feel confident that I'm ready to face the challenges in the web industry and I'm looking forward to meeting you to discuss any opportunities we might have to work together.
